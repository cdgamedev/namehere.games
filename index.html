Embark on an epic quest as Sol, a young girl who wakes from cryosleep to find her home abandoned and flooded, skyscrapers half underwater and not a soul in sight.

Explore and solve puzzles with the help of her robot companion, RoBo. Journey to the heart of the city, The Insititute Tower. It might hold the answers to all of Sol's questions - if she can reach it.
